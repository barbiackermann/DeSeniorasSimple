Señoras was born from small creative alliances that took place during 2020, until we formed the group of five that we are.

The emergence of the pandemic and isolation meant an opportunity to grow and transform ourselves, even within the challenges that are expected in that context. Distance was no longer a limit and virtuality allowed us to continue working no matter where we were.

In this new reality, digital marketing, social networks and web pages went from being a choice to the only possible way to continue with a business. Our vision is to be able to accompany whoever needs it in this process.
